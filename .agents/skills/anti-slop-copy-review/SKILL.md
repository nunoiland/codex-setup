# Anti-slop Copy Review

Use when drafting or reviewing text that users, operators, investors, or reviewers will read.

## Workflow

1. Identify the audience and trust level.
2. Mark claims that sound vague, inflated, formulaic, or unsupported.
3. Replace filler with concrete product facts.
4. Prefer active voice and specific nouns.
5. Keep regulated, financial, billing, security, and privacy copy restrained.
6. Score clarity, specificity, trust, density, and human tone from 1 to 5.
7. Return only concrete findings and suggested rewrites.

## Review rules

- Cut throat-clearing openers and meta commentary.
- Avoid dramatic contrast formulas and generic motivational phrasing.
- Remove hype unless the claim is backed by evidence.
- Use the user's terminology and repository facts.
- Do not make copy playful when the surface requires trust.
- Do not invent proof, metrics, user quotes, or guarantees.

## Output

Return:

- overall score
- findings by severity
- exact replacement suggestions
- remaining risks or unanswered proof points
